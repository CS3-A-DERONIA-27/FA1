We Bare Bears <33

  • an animated television series.
  • known for its humor, heartwarming moments, and unique premise involving three anthropomorphic bears trying to navigate the modern world.
  • Grizz is the charismatic and outgoing older brother. He's a brown bear who loves food, socializing, and trying new things. He often acts as the leader of the trio.
  • Panda is the sensitive and tech-savvy middle brother. He's a white bear with black markings and has a penchant for technology, social media, and, of course, cute things.
  • Ice Bear is the youngest brother and the most stoic of the three. He rarely speaks in the first person and often refers to himself as "Ice Bear." He's a polar bear with exceptional cooking and combat skills.
